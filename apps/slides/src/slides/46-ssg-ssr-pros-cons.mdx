## SSG & SSR Pros & Cons

- ✅ Fast first paint with real content — no spinner wall
- ✅ SEO friendly — crawlers get the full page
- ✅ SSG: served from a CDN, effectively free at runtime
- ❌ SSR: you now run (and pay for) a server — TTFB waits on your data
- ❌ You still ship all the JavaScript — hydration cost remains
- ❌ SSG: data is stale until the next build
