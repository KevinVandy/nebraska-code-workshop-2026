## What We Will _Not_ Cover

- Even though TanStack is framework agnostic, this workshop is focused on React.
- TanStack DB (It's awesome, but it's still in development)
- 3rd Party Libraries that are not TanStack
  - Our app will use Zod, ShadCN UI, and Tailwind as implementation details, but we won't focus on them
  - We can briefly discuss TanStack competitors, but we won't have learning objectives for them
- Authentication (we will fake it)
- Unit and e2e testing
- A Note on AI
  - During the first half of the Workshop, we will avoid using AI to generate code for us
  - During the second half of the Workshop, we will discuss effective AI usage and how to do it safely and effectively
